Our Mission

We started fabulous cupcakes as an attempt to make a little extra money after college. Since 2016 it has grown to be a world renwon business. We decided that we wanted to give back the joy that the world first brought us so we donate 50% of our procedes to a non profit organization that provides education and food to young children in an over all goal to raise one million dollars to this charity. By hand crafting cupcakes we provide a better life for many kids. Each month we come up with a new selection of six different cupcakes in collaboration with kids.
